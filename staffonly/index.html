Loading

Load wood, balls, etc. from shed. Load tables & chairs from ASUC Cub-E no later than 5:00 PM.

Unloading

Everything needs to be unloaded into HMMB. Typically, use front door for the field, back for tables/chairs (service elevator).

Builders

Build the field. Leads: Jenny, Alan, Grant, Charlie.

Pit Set-Up

Set up pits, tape signs, add power strips & tape down wires.

A/V

Set up speakers. Leads: Doug

Roles Day-Of

Assignments Sheet

Please check here for your assignments! These are not 100% set in stone, so feel free to talk to Vivien, Alan, Scott, or a leadership member if you don't feel comfortable working your role! If you see a role that seems to need more help, feel free to step in and give them a hand. Make sure you talk to the respective leads if you have any questions about your role.

Pits/Queueing

A wide variety of tasks. This includes supervising pits and the practice field, ensuring safety procedures are being followed (safety goggles), etc. Leads: Doug, Carlos, James

Kit Extension/Tools

Supervise kit extension and tool checkout. Leads: Jiana, James

GDC/MDC

Make sure the respective competitions run according to plan, referee, etc. Leads: Alan, Grant, Charlie

Judges/Guests

Keep the judges and other guests happy and informed! Lead: Scott

Debug

Fix the robot when the students ask. Leads: Arjun, Bob, Seiya

Field Control

Maintain robot connectivity on the field. Lead: Karthik
